Some say Love is War,
But I prefer to think
This life is war
And Love is the brink.

With each battle,
Lost or won,
I come closer to you,
My one and only sun.

For this first journey,
Simply walk.
Around the corner,
Around the block.
To the park close at hand,
For a stroll so grand.

I am sorry, my dear,
I haven't figured out
How to navigate you here,
So you may need
To use a normal map
From here, to steer.
